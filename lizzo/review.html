About Lizzo

Melissa Jefferson, famously known as Lizzo is an American singer, rapper, and actress. Lizzo is Detroit native but, she was raised in Houston, Texas. Before signing with Atlantic Records she released two studio albums in the year 2013 and 2015. This year her newest album "Cuz I Love You" blew up putting her number six on the Billboard 200 chard! Her top songs are "Truth Hurts" and "Juice"
